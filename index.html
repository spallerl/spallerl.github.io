I recently finished my postdoc at UC Berkeley. Previously, I was a Ph.D. student in CMU where I was fortunte to be advised by Venkatesan Guruswami. My research interests are in optimization and theoretical computer science broadly, approximation algorithms and hardness of approximation in particular.
Before CMU, I spent a wonderful year at Microsoft Research India as Research Fellow where I worked with Ravishankar Krishnaswamy. Prior to that, I did my undergrad from IIT Bombay.
